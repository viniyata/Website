I listen.

My research is focused on the investigation of aural ecosystems that shape and inform human behavior. My work explores the relationship of audition to the other senses - in designing both digital and physical experiences.

I am interested in acoustic ecology, insects and rituals. I enjoy systems thinking, information and content design and experiment with amalgamations of new media to re-interpret data into relevant sensory narratives. I am constantly in search of ways to enhance how we consume and propagate meaning on a daily basis.
