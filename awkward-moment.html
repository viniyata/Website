that awkward moment

Project: Self Initiated / Academic

Duration: 5 Months

Role: Concept, User Research, UX Research, UI Design, Visual Design, Information & Content Design

Tools: Adobe Creative Suite

© 2017 Viniyata Pany

A peer-centric communication strategy and web service to sensitise the Indian youth towards sexuality education. This platform works to initiate conversations between an adolescent and an older person, whom he trusts (who maybe a sibling, or a parent, or an older friend) and to equip that older person with enough information to answer the adolescents curiosity regarding sexuality issues.
