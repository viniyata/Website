The Hyper Headphones are designed to offer a sonic experience wherein one can control objects within a soundscape. The headphones respond to specific body gestures to change the 'panning' and 'volume' of the sound. When users tilt their head from left to right, the sound maps linearly to the left and right audio channel panning, while the front and back tilt changes the volume levels.
