intrea

Project: at Addikt, Mumbai

Duration: 5 Weeks

Role: Concept, Branding, Graphic Design

Tools: Adobe creative suite

Collaborator: Logo by Hoowan Ling

© 2017 Viniyata Pany

Intrea is designer Purva Kothatri's brand that specialises in bespoke diamond jewellery. Every diamond is unique. There is an intricate process that goes into polishing a rough crystal to become a precious gem. The brand language is a derivation of the crossection of a rough crystal combined with a suggestive conversion from a ragged unpolished figure to a unique shape. Five such complimenting forms were created that transtlated into the brand's language.
