kokaihop

Project: at Liquid Designs India

Duration: 5 Months

Role: Project Management, Concept, UX Design & Research, UI Design

Tools: Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch

© 2017 Viniyata Pany

Kohaihop is a recipe exploration app designed for iOS. It is a wholesome culinary experience that allows users to upload, search and categorize their favorite recipes. The app is a platform for likeminded people to share recipes and ideas about various cuisines and cooking techniques. Kokaihop aims to connect food lovers across Sweden.
