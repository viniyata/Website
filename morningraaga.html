Hyper Headphones allow you to manipulate sounds within a soundscape. They respond to body gestures to change the 'panning' and 'volume' of the sound. When users tilt their head from left to right, the sound maps linearly to the left and right audio channel panning, while the front and back tilt changes the volume levels.
