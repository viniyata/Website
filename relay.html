relay

Project: Academic

Duration: 1 Week

Role: Concept, Sound Design, Physical Computing

Tools: Arduino, Max/MSP

Collaborator: Eunjee Kim

© 2017 Viniyata Pany

Relay exhibits the chain reaction that a yawn can cause. An experiment in recording & editing sound, the device is a “collection of yawns” that would subsequently make the audience yawn. Each time the box is opened it produces a different yawn. This was done using a light sensor.
