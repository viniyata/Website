relay

Project: Academic

Duration: 1 Week

Role: Concept, Sound Design, Physical Computing

Tools: Arduino, Max/MSP

Collaborator: Eunjee Kim

© 2017 Viniyata Pany

Relay explores the chain reaction that a yawn can cause. This product is a “collection of yawns” that makes the audience yawn. Each time the box is opened it produces a different yawn.
